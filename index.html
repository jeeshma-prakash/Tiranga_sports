About Us

Igniting Rural Potential Through Sport

Tiranga Sports Foundation began with a handful of donated cones, a dusty ground, and a promise. In 2019, coaches saw young talent fade due to lack of equipment, structure, and safe spaces especially for girls. What started as weekend drills soon became a rural movement supported by communities, parents, and passionate athletes.
